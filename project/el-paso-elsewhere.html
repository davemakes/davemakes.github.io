El Paso, Elsewhere

Neo-noir horror third-person shooter

El Paso, Elsewhere is an indie horror third-person shooter developed by Strange Scaffold. Dave was brought on to design the UI, paying respect to the classic shooters that inspired the game while modernizing the aesthetic to strike a balance with the game’s contemporary lo-fi vibe. The result is sharp, angular, graphic, and stylish without drawing attention away from the explosive gameplay visuals.

STAKES. GUNS. SLOW-MO. Fight werewolves and vampires in a reality-shifting motel. Dive through barricades to escape the grasp of evil puppets. Destroy the villain you loved. A new, third-person love letter to classic shooters. Neo-noir never looked so good.

Responsibilities
